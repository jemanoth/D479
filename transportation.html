Transportation in Taniti

Explore the island with ease using convenient travel options.

Getting to Taniti

Most visitors arrive by air via Tanitiâ€™s small airport, which serves propeller planes and regional jets. Some arrive by cruise ship, docking once weekly in Yellow Leaf Bay.

Ground Transportation

  • Public Buses: Operate daily in Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m.
  • Private Buses: Serve rural areas and major attractions outside the city.
  • Taxis: Widely available in Taniti City with fair pricing.
  • Rental Cars: Offered by local agencies near the airport.
  • Bicycles: Rentable from several vendors. Helmets are required by law.
  • Hotel Shuttles: Provided by most hotels and resorts on request.

Walkability

Taniti City and Merriton Landing are flat and very pedestrian-friendly. Many tourists enjoy walking or biking between lodging and nearby beaches or restaurants.
