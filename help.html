Frequently asked questions and tips to help you plan your trip to Taniti.

FAQs

What is the best time to visit Taniti?

The dry season, from May to October, offers the best weather for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do I need a visa to visit Taniti?

Most visitors can enter Taniti visa-free for up to 30 days. Check with your countryâ€™s travel advisory before booking.

Is English spoken on the island?

Yes, English is widely spoken in Taniti City and tourist areas. Less common in rural regions.

What currency is used?

Taniti uses the U.S. Dollar (USD). Many shops also accept Euros and Yen.

Is Taniti safe?

Yes, Taniti is very safe for tourists. Pickpocketing can occur in crowded areas, so be mindful of belongings.
