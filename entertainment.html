Entertainment & Activities

Whether you're here for adventure or relaxation, Taniti has something for everyone.

Outdoor Adventures

  • Beaches: Swim, sunbathe, or enjoy water sports on pristine sandy beaches.
  • Rainforest Tours: Hike through lush jungle trails or zip-line through the canopy.
  • Volcano Excursions: Guided tours to Taniti’s active volcano for a unique, scenic experience.

Cultural Experiences

  • History Museum: Learn about Taniti’s indigenous roots and local traditions.
  • Art Galleries: Discover work by local artists inspired by the island's beauty.
  • Pubs & Microbrewery: Enjoy locally crafted drinks and Tanitian nightlife.

Family-Friendly Fun

  • Arcade & Bowling: Great for all ages, located near Taniti City center.
  • Helicopter Rides: A thrilling way to see the entire island from above.
  • New Dance Club: Experience music and dancing in the heart of Merriton Landing.
